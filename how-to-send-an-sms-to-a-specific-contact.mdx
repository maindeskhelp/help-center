---
title: "How to send an SMS to a specific contact"
mode: "wide"
---

In order to send an SMS message to specific contact, navigate to **Contacts** and click on the contact you would like to send an SMS to.

In their profile card, you will see the **Message button**.

Click on it and select **Send SMS**

Write your message and click **Send Message**.

## How to send a test message

If you would like to send a test message to make sure it looks good when received as an SMS, simply click on **Send Test**, the SMS will be sent to the test number you've set up in your Twilio account.

## How to schedule an SMS to be sent later or a specific date and time

Once your SMS message tab is open and you're done writing your message, click on the **Schedule** button and select your desired date and time and click **Save Message**.
