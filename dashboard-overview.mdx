**Understanding and managing your site's dashboard is crucial for effective website management.**

Your dashboard provides valuable insight into your website's traffic data, including the best performing pages, popular devices used for searching, and other priceless information that can aid your marketing efforts. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to find and manage your dashboard, analyze website traffic, and add admins to your website.

## \
**​Access your dashboard**

Simply go to the 'Dashboard' section as shown below :

## **Analytics**

Once in your dashboard, you'll have access to the website analytics tab where you can view hourly and daily website traffic, identify popular pages, determine the origin of your website traffic, track search queries, and analyze the types of devices your visitors use.

Additionally, you can monitor popular browsers and manage your storage and bandwidth.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-1.png)
</Frame>

By selecting the date tab, you can choose a date range to analyze your website traffic.

This information is incredibly valuable as it allows you to see what is happening on your site and who is visiting, so you can make informed decisions about how to improve your website and online presence.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-2.png)
</Frame>

## **Admins**

If you need help managing your website, you can easily add admins to assist with design and management.

Simply set admin permissions and type in their email address to send an invitation.

The change tab allows you to grant permissions for other projects or funnels you're working on or selling, so you can streamline your website management and potentially monetize your website more effectively.
