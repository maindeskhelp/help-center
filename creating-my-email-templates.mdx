## Build an email template from scratch

 

Simply go to the **Email Templates** area and click on **My Templates** -\> **Add New Template.**

## \
Duplicate an existing email template

 

In order to duplicate an existing email template, you can hover on an existing template -\> click the 3 dots -\> clone. That will create an exact replica of the email that you've copied and you can start editing it to your likes.
