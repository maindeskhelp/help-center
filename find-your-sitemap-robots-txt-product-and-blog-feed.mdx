---
title: "Find your sitemap, robots.txt, product and blog feed"
mode: "wide"
---

**Sitemap** - you can access by adding /sitemap.xml at the end of your website URL

**Blog feed** - you can access by adding /feed at the end of your website URL

**Product feed** - you can access by adding /product\_feed at the end of your website URL

**Robots.txt** - you can access by adding /robots.txt at the end of your website URL
