To connect Stripe to your website or funnel simply go to **Store** section and then click on **Settings** to enter the **Store Settings**.

Then select **Payments** and **'Add Another'** to add Stipe to your E-Commerce Store. You will be redirected to the Stripe login page. After supplying the login details, select the Account you want to connect to the Store and click Connect. That's it!

**Note:** clicking on Settings will display the option to enable Test payments. Use this to test your checkouts!

Once connected, you can login to your Stripe account and configure the payment methods you would like to offer to your Store customers!

From the list below you can enable or disable the various payment processors available to you from Stripe.

**Note:** some payment methods require certain conditions to be met in order to appear on the checkout. Such as Apple Pay. To show Apple Pay, the Visitor must access your checkout page from an Apple device with Apple Pay enabled. In all other instances, Apple Pay option will not show on your checkout.
