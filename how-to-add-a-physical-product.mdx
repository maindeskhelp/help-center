To effectively utilize the features of Maindesk, it’s important to understand both the functionality and strategic applications of each one of them. Let's delve into how you can integrate these features seamlessly into your store management process while enhancing your customer's experience.

## Add a new product

To begin creating products for your store, navigate to the 'Store' tab located at the top of your screen. From there, click on the 'Create a Product' tab, which will direct you to the product design page. This page is your creative workspace to design and specify product details—from basic descriptions to intricate customizations.

## Enter Product Title and Description

Crafting a compelling title and description for your product is crucial. These elements not only inform but also persuade potential customers. Use our AI writing assistant to enhance your product descriptions or to experiment with different tones, making your content resonate more effectively with your target audience. A well-written description improves visibility and conversion rates, making it a cornerstone of successful product listings.

## Add Widgets to Your Product Description Using Our Drag & Drop Builder

Enhance your product pages by adding interactive widgets with our easy-to-use drag & drop builder. Include elements like image galleries, videos, and FAQs to provide a richer customer experience. This not only helps in showcasing your products effectively but also in reducing queries by providing more information upfront. Widgets can significantly increase user engagement and time spent on your site, potentially boosting sales.

## Add your product category

Organizing products into categories improves user navigation and helps customers find products more efficiently. When adding a product, ensure it is correctly categorized; this helps in streamlining the customer experience and boosts SEO by grouping similar products together. Effective categorization leads to better discoverability and easier inventory management.

## Inventory

Effective inventory management is crucial for maintaining business operations and customer satisfaction. Accurately track your inventory levels to ensure that popular items are always in stock. Set up notifications for low stock levels to avoid missed sales opportunities and manage customer expectations effectively. Proper inventory management reduces the risk of overselling and helps in planning reorders timely.

## Variants

Offering product variants allows customers to choose according to their preference for size, color, or material, which can significantly enhance customer satisfaction and reduce returns. Each variant can have unique pricing, stock, and SKU numbers, which allows for detailed inventory control and tailored pricing strategies. Variants cater to diverse customer needs, making your products appealing to a broader audience.

## Custom Thank You Page, Select a Specific Thank You Page for Each Product

Customize the shopping experience by directing customers to a product-specific Thank You page after a purchase. This personal touch can make the customer feel valued and increase the likelihood of return visits. You can use this opportunity to offer related products, share exclusive discounts, or ask for feedback, which can further enhance customer engagement and loyalty.

## Additional Questions

Asking for additional information during the checkout process can cater to personalized requests like gift messages, special handling, or customization details. This feature not only enhances customer service by accommodating specific needs but also adds a personal touch to the buying process, increasing customer satisfaction and loyalty.

## Add a tag to the customer after purchasing the product.

Utilize customer tags to streamline follow-up campaigns and create targeted marketing strategies. Tags help in segmenting your customers based on their purchase behavior, making it easier to send relevant offers and communications. Effective use of tags can lead to improved marketing efficiency and increased sales through personalized engagement.

## Product SEO settings

Optimize your product pages for search engines with the help of our AI writing assistant. Incorporate targeted keywords seamlessly into your titles and descriptions to boost your search engine rankings and attract more organic traffic. Effective SEO practices can lead to greater visibility, attracting more potential customers to your store.
