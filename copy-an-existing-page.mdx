## How to Duplicate a Page

Need to create a new page that's similar to one you already have? No problem! With only 2 clicks, you can quickly copy an existing page and make any necessary changes.

## Step 1: Navigate to Pages

To begin, go to the Pages section of your website. This is where you can view and manage all of your existing pages.

## Step 2: Select the Page to Duplicate

Scroll through your list of pages until you find the one you want to duplicate. Once you've located it, hover your cursor over the page and click on the three dots that appear in the top right corner.

## Step 3: Click on Duplicate

A drop-down menu will appear when you click on the three dots. From this menu, select the option to Duplicate the page. This will create an exact copy of the page, including all of its content and settings.

## Step 4: Make Any Necessary Changes

Once the page has been duplicated, you can make any changes you need to the new page. This includes updating the page title, content, and any other settings.

And that's it! You now know how to duplicate a page on your website. This feature can save you time and effort when creating new pages that are similar to ones you already have. Happy duplicating!
