---
title: "Booking Availability"
---

To setup the Availability for an event, simply go to the **Availability** tab of that event where you can setup the availability for the operators in that meeting event.

 

 

You have the ability to:

- Select the availability for which people can schedule a meeting with that operator
- Set the day and timeframes in which the operator is available
- **Excluse days** from the availability
- Set the **increments** in which people can book an appointment
- **Schedule Notice** - to avoid a meeting in the last moment
- **Buffer** - to avoid back-to-back meetings by adding buffer between your meetings
- Maximum appointments per day
