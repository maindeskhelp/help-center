## **Introducing Individual Emailing in Maindesk**

We are excited to announce the launch of our new Individual Emailing feature in Maindesk. This feature is designed to enhance your communication strategy by allowing you to send personalized emails directly from your CRM to individual contacts. It streamlines the process of connecting with your audience on a one-to-one basis, making every interaction more meaningful.

 

## **Why Individual Emailing Matters**

- **Personalization at Your Fingertips:** Tailor each email to suit the specific needs and interests of your contacts, adding a personal touch to your communications.
- **Efficiency in Communication:** Save time by sending emails directly from your CRM without the need to set up separate campaigns for individual contacts.
- **Enhanced Engagement:** Personalized emails are more likely to be opened and read, increasing engagement and strengthening relationships with your audience.
- **Scheduling Convenience:** Schedule emails to be sent at a later date or time, ensuring your messages are delivered at the most opportune moment.

## **Getting Started with Individual Emailing**

1. **Navigate to CRM:** Log in to your Maindesk dashboard and go to the **Contacts** section.
   **Select a Contact:** Choose the contact you wish to email from your list.
   **Compose Your Email:** on the top right, click on the envelope icon to send an individual email. There, you can craft your message directly. You have the flexibility to:
   - Change the email subject.
   - Write your content in the text editor or use the advanced drag & drop email builder for HTML emails.
   - Add properties and customize according to your needs.
2. **Scheduling Your Email:** If you wish to send your email at a later time, simply use the scheduling feature. Select the date and time for your email to be sent, and Maindesk will handle the rest.
3. **Send or Schedule:** Once your email is ready, choose to send it immediately or schedule it as per your preference.

## **Accessibility for Every User**

We believe in providing value to all our users, regardless of the plan they are on. That's why the Individual Emailing feature is included in all Maindesk plans at no extra cost. This ensures that every user can benefit from personalized communication tools, helping you to connect better with your audience.
