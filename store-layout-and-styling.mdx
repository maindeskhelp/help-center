title: "Store Layout & Styling"
---



 

With our drag & drop builder, you have complete control over the layout of your store. This article will help you understand the different options available for customizing your store's layout. 

 

## Product Page Layout

 

The product page is where customers will go to view and purchase your products. With our drag & drop builder, you can easily adjust the layout of this page to suit your needs. This includes options for image positioning and product information sections. You can also incorporate dynamic elements, such as scarcity indicators, to encourage purchases.

 

## Product Layout on Listing Page

 

The listing page is where customers can browse through all of your products. With our drag & drop builder, you can choose how each product will look on this page. This allows you to create a cohesive and visually appealing browsing experience for your customers.

 

## Cart Page

 

The cart page is where customers can view all of the items they have added to their cart before proceeding to checkout. With our drag & drop builder, you can modify the look and feel of this page to match your store's branding. This includes options for changing the font and color of fields, adding tags and extra fields, and even sending customers to autoresponders.

 

## Checkout Page

 

The checkout page is where customers will complete their purchase. With our drag & drop builder, you can modify the look and feel of this page to create a seamless and user-friendly checkout process. This includes options for changing the layout, number of steps, and product summary positioning. You can also add tags and extra fields to gather more information from your customers.

 

## Cart Quick View

 

Our cart quick view feature allows customers to preview their cart by simply hovering over the cart button. This provides a convenient and efficient way for customers to keep track of their purchases while browsing your store.
