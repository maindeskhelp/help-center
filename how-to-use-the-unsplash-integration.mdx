## **Introducing Unsplash Integration on Maindesk**

We are excited to announce a new feature that enhances your creative capabilities within Maindesk – the integration of Unsplash! This integration brings a vast library of high-quality, royalty-free images directly into your Maindesk workflow, making it easier than ever to find and use stunning visuals for your projects.

\
​

## **Why Unsplash Integration is a Game-Changer:**

1. **Access to a Massive Library:** Unsplash offers a diverse collection of photos across various themes and styles, providing you with more choices to find the perfect image for your project.
2. **Streamlined Workflow:** With direct access to Unsplash from within Maindesk, you can save time and maintain your creative flow without switching between different platforms or tabs.
3. **High-Quality, Royalty-Free Images:** All Unsplash images are high-resolution and royalty-free, ensuring your projects look professional while eliminating concerns about image rights and usage fees.
4. **Enhanced Creativity:** The integration opens up new possibilities for creative expression, allowing you to easily experiment with different visuals and enhance the impact of your work.

\
​

## **Using Unsplash Integration in Maindesk:**

1. **Search for Images:** In the file manager, you'll find an option to access Unsplash **(Image Directory)**. Click on it to open the Unsplash search interface.
2. **Find the Perfect Image:** Type in keywords to search for images. For example, enter "beach" or "cityscape" to find relevant images. Browse through the results to find the one that fits your needs.
3. **Select and Optimize:** Once you find an image, select it. Maindesk will automatically handle the optimization process, resizing, compressing the image and converting it into WebP format for more image delivery and loading speed.
4. **Add to Your Project:** The selected image is now ready to be used in your project. You can also find it in your downloaded folder within Maindesk for future use.
