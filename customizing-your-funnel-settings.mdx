---
title: "Customizing your funnel settings"
---

This guide will assist you in navigating the customization options available for your funnels. By adjusting these settings, you can enhance the effectiveness of your funnel, tailor its appearance, and optimize it for both social media and search engines.

 

## Accessing Funnel Settings

 

To modify your funnel settings, click on **Funnel Settings** within your dashboard. A pop-up window will appear, presenting you with various options for customization.

 

## Basic Customizations

 

You have the option to change the name of your funnel to better reflect its purpose or contents, which is essential for identification and management. Adding a favicon that will appear in the browser tab when users visit your funnel can make your site more recognizable. Additionally, setting an image for when your funnel is shared on social media platforms can help it stand out and attract more clicks.

## \
Website Integration

 

If your funnel is part of a larger website, you have the capability to inject custom code directly through your funnel settings, integrating it seamlessly with the rest of your site. For those building a website with a funnel as the central entry point, you can set your funnel to serve as the homepage. This feature is particularly useful for conducting A/B testing on different homepage versions to determine which one yields the best performance.

## \
Advanced Customizations

 

For enhanced tracking and functionality, you can add custom scripts like analytics or pixel trackers to your funnel’s head section via the custom head markup feature. To tailor the visual styling of your funnel and ensure it matches your brand or specific design requirements, you can insert custom CSS. Furthermore, the custom body markup allows the addition of scripts, styles, or other HTML elements directly into the body of your funnel’s pages, providing even greater flexibility and customization.

 

## \
Use Cases and Ideas

Enhancing the user experience through custom CSS can make your funnel more engaging and visually appealing to visitors. Integrating various analytics tools through custom head markup enables you to gather valuable data on funnel performance and visitor interactions, which is crucial for optimizing your marketing strategies. Additionally, connecting a custom domain and setting up a favicon and social sharing images help maintain branding consistency across all customer touchpoints.
