---
title: "How to send a voice message to a specific contact"
mode: "wide"
---

In order to send an voice message to specific contact, navigate to **Contacts** and click on the contact you would like to send the voice message to.

In their profile card, you will see the **Message button**.

Click on it and select **Send Voice**

Now, you have 2 options :

- Send a voice message using AI text to speech
- Upload your own pre-recorded voice message

## Send a voice message using text to speech

In order to generate a voice message for free, you need to write your text, choose a language and a voice from the free available voices :

- Man - Male
- Woman - Female

Other more advanced voices are available but Twilio will charge an extra fee for them

## Send a voice message using your own pre-recorded voice message

In order to send your own pre-recorded message, just click on **Attach** - **Attach Audio** and upload your **MP3 file**.
