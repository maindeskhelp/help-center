---
title: "Create A Booking Service"
---

To create a booking service go to Booking -\> Services -\> **Create New Service**

You have the ability to add:

- Name of the service
- Description
- Type of the service - Group or One-on-One. If you select a Group Meeting, that allows multiple people to book for the same slot
- Duration of the meeting
- Image (shown if you have a choice of services)
- Tag - to tag users who book for that meeting
