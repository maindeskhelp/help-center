Adding Fonts to Your Website

Fonts are an important part of your website's design. They can help convey your brand's personality and make your content more visually appealing. In this article, we will show you how to add fonts to your website using our Global Styling tab.

## Navigating to the Global Styling Tab

To add fonts to your website, you will need to navigate to the Global Styling tab. This can be found in the settings or customization section of your website's dashboard.

## Selecting Fonts

Once you are in the Global Styling tab, you will see an option to "Add Fonts." Click on this to begin adding fonts to your website.

There are two options for adding fonts: "add a new font" or "upload custom font."

## Adding a New Font

If you choose to add a new font, you will have access to over 1000 font types available on Google Fonts. This is a great option if you are looking for a specific font or want to explore different options.

You can select up to 8 font types at one time, but we recommend limiting your selection to 3 to maintain consistency with your brand and site performance.

## Uploading a Custom Font

If you have a specific font that you want to use on your website, you can upload it as a custom font. To do this, select "upload custom font" and be sure to give it a name for easy reference.

Once uploaded, click "done" and your custom font will be available to use across your entire site.

## Conclusion

Adding fonts to your website is a simple process that can greatly enhance your website's design. Whether you choose to add a new font or upload a custom one, make sure to select fonts that align with your brand and maintain consistency throughout your site.
