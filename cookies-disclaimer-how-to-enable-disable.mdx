title: "Cookies disclaimer - how to enable/disable"
mode: "wide"
---

Enabling a cookie disclaimer is super simple. Go to your Website Settings, click on Cookies & Privacy and enable the Cookie Disclaimer for your website.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-17.png)
</Frame>

\
You can edit the raw HTML markup of the disclaimer shown to your customers, and provide a link to your Terms & Conditions / Cookie Policy pages.
