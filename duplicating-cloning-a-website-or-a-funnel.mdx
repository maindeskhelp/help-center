**To duplicate a website or a funnel**, go to the Funnels & Websites section of your project to see all the sites and funnels built in your current project. This is the button on the top-le

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-28.png)
</Frame>

Then go with your mouse on top of the funnel or the website that you want to to clone and you will see the 3 dots on the right:

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-29.png)
</Frame>

And select the **Duplicate** option from the list.
