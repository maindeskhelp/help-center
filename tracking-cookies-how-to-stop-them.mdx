title: "Tracking cookies - how to stop them"
---

We've created a special System popup that allows your website visitors to disable the tracking cookies from the builder. You can find them when you link a popup at the very bottom.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-17.png)
</Frame>

The click would trigger a popup with the option to disable/enable the tracking cookies initiated from the website builder.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-18.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
  **Note:** This setting will only disable the website builder's tracking and NOT any other tracking software on the website.
</Info>
