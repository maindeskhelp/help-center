---
title: "Creating An Email Marketing Segment"
mode: "wide"
---

To create your segments go to your **Emails & Automations** tab and click on **Lists and Subscribers.**

After that click on **Add Filter** on top of your screen to start adding filters to define your Segments\*\*.\*\* You can create as many as you want segments based on different criteria. You can also update an existing segment.

To view all Segments simply click on the **Segments** tab next to Lists.
